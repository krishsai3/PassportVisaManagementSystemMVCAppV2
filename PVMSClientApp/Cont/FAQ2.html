As a government employee, I currently possess a visa that remains valid for another 8 months. However, I am contemplating the possibility of canceling this visa before its expiration. Could you please provide information about the associated costs and procedures for canceling the visa?

Given your status as a Government Employee, the cost for canceling your visa depends on its remaining validity. If your visa is valid for less than 6 months, the cancellation fee amounts to 2800 rupees. For visas with validity exceeding 6 months but not extending beyond 12 months, the cancellation charge stands at 2500 rupees. In the case of visas with a validity surpassing 12 months, the cancellation fee is reduced to 1500 rupees. It's important to factor in these charges when considering the cancellation of your visa

What do I do if I lose my passport?

Loss of passport should be immediately reported to the nearest Police Station and to the Passport Office or Indian Mission, if abroad. If required, you can apply for Passport Renewal by paying 1200 rupees. It takes around 12 days to issue your passport
